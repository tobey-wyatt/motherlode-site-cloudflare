Your audience is full of founders who can't figure out why they're still grinding. Revenue is up. The team is in place. And everything still lands on their desk. Their fixes work for a few months, then break. They're starting to wonder if they're the problem.

They're not. They're too close to see what is.

Tobey Wyatt coaches founder-CEOs through the shift from operator to diagnostic thinker. Her talks are the first time most founders in the room hear someone describe their Tuesday and realize they're not alone in it.

Your audience walks away with:

  • ✦ The relief of finally hearing their problem named accurately
  • ✦ The difference between what's on fire and what's actually broken
  • ✦ A diagnostic instinct they can use the next morning, not just the next quarter
